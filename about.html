About me

I didn't read much growing up. My parents spoke Spanish but my father
insisted we speak English at home. He spoke Spanish but spoke English at work.
We didn't have books around the house and we didn't go to the library.
I learned to read from a neighbor who was two years younger than I.
Her parents bought her books to read. In the summer, we spent most mornings
reading together. She moved away when I was 10 years old.

I stopped trying to read school textbooks after the 4th grade. I simply didn't
have the language skills or the vocabulary. I chose a profession that prioritized
math and science because I learned to understand those symbols, equations, and concepts.
After I had children, I came across a snippet of a poem while they were in preschool.
I looked it up and it inspired me. (It is listed under the "Site Navigation List".)
I read many books to them and as they learned to read, so did I.
Later, I took some online classes in psychology and I learn how to write effectively.

Now, reading is my superpower and I am still making up for lost time.
