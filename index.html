It feels very strange to write about reading since I typically don't give it much thought except
when I am picking out new books to read.

I like reading because books are cheap and I am in control of what I read. Unlike other pleasures,
reading is a pleasure which has not diminished over time. I especially love reading in bed.
It is the best way for me to unwind and end a day.
To me, a book is a portal to other experiences, other people,
other worlds, other universes, and even other timelines.
Books let my imagination run free.
